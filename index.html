About Chennai

Chennai, formerly known as Madras, is the capital city of Tamil Nadu, located on the Coromandel Coast of the Bay of Bengal. It is one of the largest cultural, economic and educational centers in South India.

Known as the "Detroit of India" for its automobile industry, Chennai is also famous for its rich culture, temples, and vibrant arts scene.

Chennai Highlights

  • Marina Beach - Second longest urban beach in the world
  • Kapaleeshwarar Temple - Ancient Shiva temple
  • Fort St. George - First English fortress in India
  • San Thome Basilica - Built over the tomb of St. Thomas
  • Government Museum - Second oldest museum in India
  • Kalakshetra Foundation - Centre for Indian classical arts
